## 7. Instructor Presentation (Suggested)

> We use the **Zhang et al. (2017)** Beijing hourly benchmark—not MNIST or a Kaggle tabular set. Our five-block model combines a denoising autoencoder, CNN, BiLSTM, residual fusion, and self-attention. After Optuna tuning, Model A reaches **$R^2 \approx 0.92$** on clean data and **retains $R^2 \approx 0.92$ under sensor noise**, while the no-DAE variant drops to **$R^2 \approx 0.87$**—a **+0.05** robustness margin. The full write-up, tables, and 300 DPI figures are in `README.md` and `outputs/`.
